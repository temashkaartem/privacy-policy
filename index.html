Privacy Policy

The app does not require manual user registration and does not ask users to provide personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

The application uses Google Play Games Services for optional user sign-in and achievement functionality.

Google Play Games Services may collect and process certain data (such as a player ID, achievements, and sign-in information) in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy. This data is handled exclusively by Google, and the developer does not have access to or store this information.

The application does not use advertising services, in-app analytics, or tracking technologies beyond those provided by Google Play Games Services.

Download statistics and general information such as the number of installs are provided only by Google Play and are processed according to Google’s own privacy policies.

The app is designed for educational and entertainment purposes and is suitable for children.
